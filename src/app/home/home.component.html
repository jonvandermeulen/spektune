Spektune is a collaborative project that turns data into music.

We have collected data from NASA satellites that monitor earth's oceans, and have applied musical theory to the data to create sonifications that express the natural harmonies that exist in nature.

 

In this example, listen as we travel across the Rio de la Plata estuary in the southwest Atlantic Ocean. The patterns you hear in the music are the same patterns that enable scientists to unravel the microscopic composition of the ocean from space.

 

Listen to the seasons change in this ambient soundscape taken from a year-long survey of the Coral Sea off the coast of Queensland, Australia

 

Make your own sonifications!

Spektune Composer help you make sonifications of your own. Simply input your data into tracks and configure the musical rules to apply to it. You can preview the tracks in the tool, but it really shines when you export a MIDI file and bring it into musical production software like Garage Band, Logic Pro, Fruity Loops and the like.
